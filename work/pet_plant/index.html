Welcome to the Pet Plant Project

The Pet Plant app aims to cultivate children’s environmental awareness by supporting them the process of taking care of a plant with a micro:bit monitoring the plant’s conditions.

Since plants don’t talk, we understand that children might not know about how their plant is feeling.

The Pet Plant app, in collaboration with a micro:bit, displays the conditions of their plant.

From our user experience research, children expressed their desire to see a pet, so we let children choose a pet as the avatar of their plant, in the hope of building children’s emotional attachment towards the plant.

To achieve the educational goals, the app offers a brief introduction to each plant in the plant selection interface.

The app interface offers multiple pet options to let children choose a pet among several choices.
